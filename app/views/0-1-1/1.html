Estimate your Income Tax for the current year

Use this service to estimate your take-home pay and how much Income Tax and National Insurance you should pay for the current tax year (6 April 2023 to 5 April 2024).

You may be able to claim a refund if you've paid too much tax.

Before you start

You cannot get an estimate online if:

  • you're not a Pay As You Earn (PAYE) taxpayer
  • your only income is from self employment or state benefits, such as State Pension
{% from "govuk/components/inset-text/macro.njk" import govukInsetText %} {{ govukInsetText({ text: "If you have more than one job, use the calculator once for each job." }) }}

You can also use the tax calculator in the HMRC app.

There are different ways to:

  • check your Income Tax payments for the current year
  • check how much you paid last year (6 April 2022 to 5 April 2023)
  • estimate how much you should have paid in a previous year
  • budget for your tax bill if you're self employed

You can check you've paid the right tax by contacting HMRC or by getting help from an accountant.
